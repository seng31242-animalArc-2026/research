## Purpose

The purpose of this survey is to collect requirements for the proposed Animal Ark street animal welfare management system.

The survey was used to identify current workflow problems and required system features related to:

- Street animal treatment management
- Volunteer doctor/student coordination
- Shift handover and treatment continuity
- Emergency awareness and notifications
- Medicine inventory management
- Donation support
- Adoption workflow
- Public engagement

---

## Survey Introduction

Thank you for participating in this survey.

This survey is conducted to collect requirements for the development of Animal Ark, an open-source system designed to help animal hospitals and welfare organizations manage street animal treatment, volunteer doctor coordination, medicine inventory, donor support, and adoption workflows.

The organization currently manages most operations manually, and this survey aims to identify the most important problems and required features.

## Section 6 — Public / Donors

**Section description:** This section is for the public, donors, adopters, and animal lovers who may support or engage with Animal Ark.

Animal Ark is a student-led animal welfare society at the University of Peradeniya that rescues, treats, and helps stray or injured animals find safe homes.

The proposed Animal Ark system aims to improve treatment coordination, donations, emergency support, adoptions, and public engagement.
